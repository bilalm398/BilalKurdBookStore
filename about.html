About Kurdish BookStore

We are passionate about bringing the joy of reading to every home in Kurdistan and beyond.

📚

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Kurdish BookStore started with a simple mission: to make Kurdish literature and books accessible to everyone. We believe that books have the power to transform lives and communities.

From our humble beginnings as a small local bookstore, we've grown into one of the leading online book retailers serving Kurdish readers worldwide.

Today, we offer thousands of titles in Kurdish, English, Arabic, and many other languages, carefully curated to meet the diverse interests of our readers.
